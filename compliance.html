Safety & Compliance

Platform Safety and Compliance Checklist

This page explains the practical safety and compliance steps QuickTrip expects for platform use. It is not legal advice and does not replace any requirement under applicable law.

1. Driver Document Readiness

  • valid driving licence
  • vehicle registration certificate
  • active insurance
  • PUC certificate
  • permit or fitness documents where applicable

2. Booking Record Keeping

QuickTrip should keep basic booking records such as trip date, route details, driver details, and communication logs where reasonably necessary for support, complaint review, and platform record keeping.

3. Privacy and Consent

Customers should be informed how their booking details are used, and booking details should be handled consistently with the Privacy Policy and applicable law.

4. Platform Limits

Nothing on the website should be read as government approval, legal exemption, or a substitute for any licence, permit, registration, tax, or compliance requirement that may apply to drivers, vehicle owners, or platform activity.

5. Contact

For compliance-related questions, contact QuickTrip at +91 84097 58097.
