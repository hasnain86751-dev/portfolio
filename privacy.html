How QuickTrip Uses Booking Information

This policy explains what personal information QuickTrip collects, why it is collected, and how it is used for customer-driver booking communication.

Effective date: March 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

QuickTrip may collect the information you provide during booking or communication, including your name, phone number, pickup location, drop location, travel date, preferred car, and other travel details you choose to share.

2. How We Use Information

We use booking information to respond to customer inquiries, help pass booking requests to available independent drivers, coordinate trip details, provide support, maintain records, and improve the booking experience.

3. Sharing of Information

QuickTrip may share relevant booking details with independent drivers so they can review and respond to a travel request. We may also share information when required by applicable law, legal process, or lawful government request.

4. WhatsApp and Third-Party Communication

Many bookings are handled through WhatsApp. When you contact QuickTrip through WhatsApp, your communication is also subject to WhatsApp's own terms and privacy practices.

5. Data Retention

QuickTrip keeps booking information only for as long as reasonably necessary for communication, trip coordination, record-keeping, dispute handling, safety, and legal compliance.

6. Your Choices

You may choose not to share certain information, but some details are necessary to process a booking request. You may contact QuickTrip to request correction or deletion of information, subject to legal or operational requirements.

7. Platform Role

QuickTrip is a technology-enabled booking and communication platform that helps customers connect with independent drivers for personal travel. QuickTrip does not own, lease, or operate vehicles, does not employ drivers as transport operators, and does not directly provide taxi or transportation services.

8. Security

QuickTrip uses reasonable administrative and practical measures to protect booking information, but no website, device, or messaging platform can guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

QuickTrip may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will apply from the date shown on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy or booking questions, contact QuickTrip through WhatsApp at +91 84097 58097 or by phone at +91 84097 58097.

If you want correction or deletion of information, contact QuickTrip with enough details to identify your booking request.
