QuickTrip Terms for Customers and Drivers

These terms explain how bookings, communication, responsibilities, and platform use work on QuickTrip.

Effective date: March 23, 2026

1. Platform Role

QuickTrip is a technology-enabled booking and communication platform that helps customers connect with independent drivers for personal travel. QuickTrip does not own, lease, or operate vehicles, does not employ drivers as transport operators, and does not directly provide taxi or transportation services.

2. Booking Requests

When a user submits a booking request through WhatsApp or the website, QuickTrip may share the request details with an available independent driver. A booking is confirmed only after the customer and the independent driver agree on availability, route, pricing, and any applicable charges.

3. Driver Responsibility

Drivers using QuickTrip remain independently responsible for:

  • vehicle permits, insurance, taxes, and fitness
  • driving licence validity and legal eligibility to drive
  • compliance with applicable transport, motor vehicle, and local laws
  • vehicle condition, punctuality, and customer conduct during the trip

QuickTrip may request supporting driver or vehicle documents before sharing trip leads and may suspend or remove drivers if documents appear invalid, incomplete, or outdated.

4. Customer Responsibility

Customers agree to provide accurate booking details, use the service for lawful personal travel, behave respectfully with drivers, and pay the agreed fare or charges directly as mutually decided with the driver unless stated otherwise.

5. Pricing and Payments

QuickTrip may display or communicate estimated pricing, but final pricing, extra charges, waiting charges, tolls, parking, and route-specific costs may depend on the agreement between the customer and the independent driver.

6. Cancellations and Delays

Trip timing may be affected by traffic, weather, vehicle issues, or driver availability. Customers and drivers should inform each other promptly in case of delay or cancellation. Cancellation charges, if any, should be clearly communicated before confirmation.

7. Platform Limits

QuickTrip acts only as a communication and booking facilitator. QuickTrip does not by this website claim any government approval, exemption, or waiver from licensing or legal requirements, and nothing on the website should be read as a substitute for compliance obligations that may apply under transport, motor vehicle, tax, consumer, privacy, or other applicable laws.

QuickTrip is not responsible for acts, omissions, route disputes, traffic violations, accidents, vehicle defects, or losses caused by independent drivers or third parties, except to the extent required by applicable law.

8. Prohibited Use

Users may not use the website or booking channel for unlawful transport activity, false bookings, spam, harassment, fraud, or any purpose that violates applicable law or the rights of others.
