Grievance Support

Complaints, Disputes, and Support

If you have a complaint about booking communication, customer conduct, driver conduct, pricing disputes, or privacy concerns, contact QuickTrip through the channels below.

1. How to Raise a Complaint

You can raise a complaint through WhatsApp or phone using the contact number listed on the website. Please include your name, phone number, trip date, route, and a short description of the issue.

2. Types of Issues You Can Report

  • driver or customer behaviour concerns
  • booking communication issues
  • route or pricing disputes
  • privacy or data concerns
  • incorrect listing or vehicle information

3. Platform Role in Complaints

QuickTrip may review complaints related to communication and listing activity on the platform. QuickTrip may also contact the relevant driver or customer for clarification and may suspend access to trip leads where necessary.
