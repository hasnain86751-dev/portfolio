Quick answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Payment kaise hoga?

Trip payment driver confirmation ke baad mutually coordinated hota hai. Fare, toll, parking, waiting, aur route conditions ke hisab se final amount decide kiya ja sakta hai.

Driver kab confirm hoga?

Booking request WhatsApp se bhejne ke baad QuickTrip available independent driver ke saath coordinate karta hai. Confirmation usually route, time, aur car availability check karne ke baad diya jata hai.

Outstation booking possible hai?

Haan, outstation trips possible hain subject to driver availability, route plan, and trip timing. Long-distance booking ke liye pehle se request bhejna better rahega.

Night travel available hai?

Night travel selected routes par available ho sakta hai, lekin ye driver availability aur safety considerations par depend karega. Immediate confirmation ke liye call ya WhatsApp best rahega.
